HOW TO PLAY

[ ENGLISH INSTRUCTIONS ]

1. The entire map is pitch black. You cannot see the walls normally.
2. Moving around creates Soundwaves (Sonar pulses) that briefly reveal the environment and enemy locations.
3. Avoid the red enemy guard at all costs. If they touch you, your data gets wiped.
4. Navigate through the maze and reach the Golden Exit Portal located on the right side.
5. Controls: Use WASD, Arrow Keys or Mobile On-Screen buttons to move.
